Who I am

Hi, I’m Vivek — a Computer Science student with a passion for game development and web development. I enjoy building interactive, user-focused projects that blend creativity and technology. When I’m not coding, I’m usually playing guitar or exploring new video games, both of which inspire my approach to design and development.

How I work

I follow a user-centered, iterative design process. I start by creating wireframes and prototypes using Figma to explore ideas and plan layouts. Then, I develop functional versions with code, refining the design based on feedback and testing. Currently, I’m deepening my UI/UX knowledge through SEG3525, applying best practices to build clear and engaging interfaces.
